Projects

The Effects of Targeted Advertising on Stigmatized Health Identities: Engagement algorithms optimize content promotion based on the behavioral histories and demographic profiles of individuals. As the personal data economy grows, user data is continuously commodified at scale. I investigate how this new system of monetization creates a unique set of harms for individuals who could be vulnerable to repeated exposure to potentially harmful content.

Genealogies of Datasets : Data collection and dataset construction are both integral and often understudied aspects of machine learning. My research investigates the politics of collecting, cleaning and labeling data. I'm primarily interested in understanding the role of curation in the reinforcement of systemic inequality within sociotechnical systems.

Critical Theory & Practice: So we've discovered that technology has disparate effects on society. What happens next? Taking into account the significance of critical theories such as critical race theory, feminist theory and indigenous scholarship-- I aim to find ways for these theories to influence the way engineers, designers and researchers create data products.
